# KCET Predictor

## Problem Statement

Every year thousands of students appear for the Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) and receive a rank after the examination.

After obtaining their rank, students face several challenges:

* Determining which colleges they can realistically get.
* Understanding branch-wise cutoff trends.
* Comparing colleges across different cities.
* Identifying dream, reach, safe, and guaranteed options.
* Deciding whether to participate in later counselling rounds.
* Filtering colleges based on location and branch preference.

Existing solutions are often:

* Outdated
* Difficult to use
* Limited in filtering options
* Based on incomplete data
* Unable to provide personalized recommendations

## Proposed Solution

KCET Predictor is a data-driven web platform that uses official KEA counselling cutoff data to provide personalized college recommendations.

The platform allows students to:

* Enter their KCET rank
* Select their reservation category
* Choose preferred branches
* Filter colleges by city and type
* View predicted admission possibilities

The system categorizes recommendations into:

* Dream Colleges
* Reach Colleges
* Safe Colleges
* Guaranteed Colleges

based on historical cutoff analysis.

## Target Users

Primary Users:

* KCET Students
* Parents
* Career Counselors

Secondary Users:

* Educational Institutions
* Placement and Admission Consultants

## Objectives

* Simplify the counselling process.
* Provide accurate and transparent recommendations.
* Reduce confusion during option entry.
* Make official cutoff information easier to understand.
* Help students make informed decisions.

## Dataset

Source:
KEA UGCET 2025 Round 3 Official Cutoff Data

Statistics:

* 229 Colleges
* 104 Branches
* 28 Reservation Categories
* 10,949 Cutoff Records

Validated and Verified
